# CookingUp 🍳

**CookingUp** is a 100% offline-first, privacy-focused recipe manager for Android. Organize your culinary library, scale ingredient quantities instantly, and share recipes with friends—all without an internet connection or cloud tracking.

## 🚀 Key Features

- **Offline-First**: No internet required. Your data never leaves your device.
- **Recipe Scaling**: Automatically adjust ingredient amounts based on your desired serving size.
- **Privacy-Centric**: Zero telemetry, zero analytics, and zero cloud sync.
- **Smart Sharing**: Export individual recipes (`.curcp`) or your entire library (`.culbr`) via Android's native sharing system.
- **OCR Scanning**: (Optional) Use your camera to scan physical recipes into digital format using on-device ML Kit.
- **Modern UI**: Built with Jetpack Compose with full Edge-to-Edge support and Material 3 design.

## 📱 Minimum Requirements

- **Operating System**: Android 7.0 (Nougat) / API level 24 or higher.
- **Hardware**: Camera (optional, for OCR scanning).
- **Storage**: Minimal space for the app and your local recipe database.

## 🛠️ Technical Details

- **Language**: Kotlin 2.1+
- **UI Framework**: Jetpack Compose
- **Database**: Room (Local)
- **Target SDK**: 36 (Android 16 "Baklava")
